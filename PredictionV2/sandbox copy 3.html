At PRC, we're dedicated to growing the RC Community by bringing a quick fun game to a live steamed event!

Our mission is to unite RC enthusiasts, whether they're seasoned veterans or newcomers, by providing an engaging platform where they can participate in thrilling RC competitions, connect with like-minded individuals, and foster a sense of camaraderie within the community. Through our innovative approach, we aim to enhance the excitement of live-streamed events and contribute to the overall growth and enjoyment of the RC hobby worldwide. Community growth is essential for the vitality and sustainability of any hobby or interest group, including the RC (Remote Control) community.
